Overview of Spy X Family

Spy X Family is a popular manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuya Endo. Serialized in Shueisha's Shōnen Jump+ magazine since March 2019, it has captured the hearts of readers with its unique blend of action, comedy, and heartfelt moments. The series has also been adapted into an anime, which premiered in April 2022.

Premise

Set in a fictional European country amid a cold war, Spy X Family follows the story of Loid Forger, a highly skilled spy known by the codename "Twilight." To maintain peace and prevent a looming conflict, Loid is tasked with a critical mission: infiltrate an elite school to get close to a high-profile target. To do this, he must create a fake family.
